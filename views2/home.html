SKCAC Required Participant Information Update


Dear participant:

Please take the time to fill out all forms as these are required to be updated annually for participants of SKCAC programs. This is a requirement of SKCAC’s contracts with King County and the State of Washington.

If you do not live with your guardian, we will send the completed packet on to your guardian for signatures. Please make sure to provide the guardian name and address on the Medical and Emergency Information sheet.

Release of Information – Please complete with names and/or agencies to which SKCAC may need to be in contact with regarding you and your program services. Some information has been completed, please feel free to correct and add.

Medical and Emergency Information – Please complete fully so SKCAC has the most current, up-to-date information in case of an emergency. Also please provide updates throughout the year as your medications and other information changes. Providing your e-mail address will help us to provide you with information regarding SKCAC events and updates.

Notice of Rights – This states the rights you are entitled to from SKCAC as a participant of our employment services.

Title II and SSI Statement Acknowledgement – This form refers you to Social Security benefits at www.ssa.gov to learn more about how working effects your Social Security benefits.

SKCAC Handbook – Please sign the last page of the handbook and send back as acknowledgment that you received the handbook.

Appeal/Grievance Procedure – please sign and return this form to SKCAC, the Appeal/Grievance Procedure is listed in your SKCAC Handbook for your records.

Paystub-- We need a copy of a recent paystub for employment verification. If your paycheck is from SKCAC you do not have to provide a copy.

Thank you for taking time to review, sign and return all the forms to SKCAC as soon as possible.

I am available to assist you in the process of getting these forms completed. Please let me know if you have any questions or need assistance.
